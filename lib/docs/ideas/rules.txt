Common Stats for All Characters:-

Strength (STR): Determines physical power and melee attack damage.
Dexterity (DEX): Affects agility, ranged attack accuracy, and evasion.
Intelligence (INT): Influences magical attack power, spell effectiveness, and mana.
Wisdom (WIS): Impacts healing abilities, magic resistance, and perception.
Constitution (CON): Affects health points and resilience to damage.
Charisma (CHA): Influences negotiation skills, leadership, and charm effectiveness.

Speed: Affects movement and action speed.
Gravity: Determines character interaction with the environment.
HP: Total health points; 0 HP means incapacitation.
MP: Mana points required for casting spells or using certain skills.
